Interstellar

Description

Interstellar is a 2014 epic science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. Interstellar follows a group of astronauts who embark on a daring mission through a wormhole near Saturn to find a new habitable planet for humanity. Led by Cooper, they navigate through cosmic anomalies and time dilation while grappling with the emotional toll of leaving their loved ones behind. The film explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the resilience of the human spirit in the face of existential challenges.
