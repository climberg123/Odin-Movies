Shutter Island

Description

Shutter Island is a 2010 American neo-noir psychological thriller film directed by Martin Scorsese. Shutter Island unfolds as U.S. Marshal Teddy Daniels and his partner Chuck investigate the disappearance of a patient from a secluded psychiatric facility on Shutter Island. As they delve deeper into the asylum's mysteries, Teddy grapples with his own haunted past and questions the reality of his surroundings. The film culminates in a mind-bending revelation that challenges perceptions of sanity and identity.
